Signal Flow

A visual study of signal and interference — projected patterns pulsing between order and noise.

Date October 2024
Role Artist & Designer
Motion DesignTouchDesignerVideo ProjectionsArtworks

About

Signal Flow traces the path of data as it degrades, transforms, and reassembles. Built in TouchDesigner, the piece visualizes the invisible architecture of signal processing — where clean information dissolves into texture and noise becomes its own kind of beauty.
