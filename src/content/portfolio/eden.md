## About

Eden is a stop-motion piece created from the artwork "La foresta nel mare" (2019) by artist Luigina Mazzocca.

**Camera & Stop-motion:** Leonardo Castellano

## Concept

The video brings the static artwork to life, revealing the hidden movements and rhythms within the original piece through careful frame-by-frame animation.
