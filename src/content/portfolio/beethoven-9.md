## About

Video projections designed for concert performances of Beethoven's Ninth Symphony. The visual design spans all four movements, building toward the triumphant "Ode to Joy" finale.

## Approach

The projections respect the primacy of the music while adding a visual dimension that can enhance the audience's experience. The imagery evolves from abstract, atmospheric visuals in the opening movements to more defined, celebratory elements in the finale.
