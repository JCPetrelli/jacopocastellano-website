## About

An edited interview with Nacho Duato, one of the most influential contemporary choreographers. The Spanish-born artist has led major ballet companies worldwide and created an extensive repertoire of works that blend classical technique with contemporary movement.

## Approach

The edit balances the intimacy of the interview format with visual elements that reflect Duato's artistic vision, incorporating motion graphics that complement rather than distract from his words.
