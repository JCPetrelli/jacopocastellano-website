## About

Licht (Light) is an experimental video piece exploring the fundamental properties of light—how it moves, refracts, and transforms space.

The work emerged from a period of intensive study into light behavior, combining practical photography with digital processing to create a meditation on illumination itself.

## Approach

Rather than documenting light sources, the piece attempts to capture light as a subject in its own right—its weight, its texture, its capacity to define and dissolve form.
