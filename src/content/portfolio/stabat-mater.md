## About the Production

Stabat Mater premiered in October 2015 at the Magdeburg Opera Haus, bringing together Rossini's sacred masterwork with contemporary ballet and immersive video projections.

## Cast

- **Sopran:** Hale Soner
- **Mezzo:** Sylvia Rena Ziegler
- **Tenor:** Felipe Rojas Velozo
- **Bass:** Martin Jan-Nijhof
- Ballett Magdeburg
- Opernchor des Theaters Magdeburg
- Magdeburgische Philharmonie

**Choreography:** Gonzalo Galguera

## Production Scope

The production involved creating 73 individual video sequences, each precisely synchronized with the live orchestra, choir, and ballet. The entire video design process spanned 10-12 months, requiring careful coordination with all performing forces.

## Inflammatus et accensus

One of the most striking movements features the "Inflammatus et accensus" sequence, where the video projections create an intense dialogue with the dramatic vocal and orchestral forces.
