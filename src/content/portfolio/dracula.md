## About the Production

Dracula premiered on April 6th, 2019 at the Magdeburg Opera Haus. This full-length ballet brings Bram Stoker's gothic masterpiece to life through an innovative combination of live performance and synchronized video projections.

## Cast

- **Mihael Belilov** as Dracula
- **Anastasia Gavrilenkova** as Lucy
- **Grettel Morejón** as Mina
- **Adrián Román Ventura** as Jonathan
- Ballett Magdeburg
- Magdeburgische Philharmonie

**Choreography:** Gonzalo Galguera

## Music

The score combines works by Sergej Rachmaninow, Jean Sibelius, Ralph Vaughan Williams, Edward Elgar, Elizabeth Maconchy, George Lloyd, Franz Schreker, and Leon Gurvitch.

## Technical Approach

The video projections feature pre-recorded choreographic footage shot in 4K at 60 fps, which is synchronized live during performances. This approach creates a dialogue between the physical dancers on stage and their projected counterparts, enhancing the supernatural atmosphere of the narrative.
