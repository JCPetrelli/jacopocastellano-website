## About

Video projections created for a performance of Giovanni Bottesini's Gran Duo Concertante, a virtuosic work showcasing the dialogue between violin and double bass with orchestral accompaniment.

The visual design complements the romantic character of the music, creating an atmospheric backdrop that enhances the concert experience without overshadowing the performers.
